# Anthropology Work

In my exploration of anthropology, I focus on the cultural practices of various communities. My work includes ethnographic research and community engagement projects.

## Research Interests
- **Cultural Food Practices**: Studying the role of food in cultural identity.
- **Community Engagement**: Working with local communities to understand their needs and perspectives.

### Projects
- **Telugu Community Ethnography**: Conducted research on cultural practices and language use in the Telugu community.
- **Culinary Anthropology**: Exploring how traditional recipes and foodways reflect cultural values.
