Ethnographic Research/Anthropology

My ethnography project explores the cultural and linguistic evolution of the Telugu community in my local area. Through fieldwork, I documented generational differences and cultural practices, highlighting how Telugu Americans maintain their heritage while adapting to American society. It discusses topics such as gender roles, Hinduism, food, gift-giving, and more, through participant observation, field notes, and interviews. Please click the pdf below to access it.

Sudden Cardiac-death Awareness Research Foundation (SCARF)

During my internship with SCARF, I researched Sudden Cardiac Death prevention and worked on public health initiatives to spread awareness, specifically in terms of Ethnic Women. My group was able to work closely with cardiologist Dr. Paul Nguyen and various stakeholders including owners of gyms and hospitals. We developed a poster project titled Empower with C.A.R.E, and after a 6-week internship, we presented our findings to a panel of judges. Ultimately, we won the Community Outreach Award ($500+ grant) for our project!
